Our Story

Over 25 Years of Logistics Excellence

Established in 2001, Logistica Global began with a singular focus: simplifying customs clearance and local transport. Over the years, through a commitment to excellence and strategic acquisitions, we expanded into a global logistics partner operating in over 150 countries.

Today, we are proud to offer fully integrated 3PL and 4PL logistics solutions, customs brokerages, temperature-controlled distribution systems, and specialized industry cargo management.

Our Mission

To deliver speed, safety, and transparency, ensuring client logistics operate smoothly.

Our Vision

To lead the transition to green, sustainable logistics via smart tech optimization.
